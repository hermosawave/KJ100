Kyoto Journal is in crisis.

The pandemic has forced Kyoto Journal to suspend its print edition (again) for the time being. Our all volunteer team worked extremely hard to bring the print edition back in 2017 after the passing of our long-time sponsor, and managed to produce 9 beautiful issues without any one regular source of funding. This development has been demoralizing, but at the same time we are determined to keep going.

Please consider supporting us today with a small contribution. It will make a huge difference!
